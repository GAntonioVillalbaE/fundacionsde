Our Mission

We are a Foundation based in the city of Cartagena de Indias (Colombia) that works to improve the quality of life of the most vulnerable children in this city, through activities that contribute to their integral development through four axes of intervention which are:

  • 1. School Accompaniment
  • 2. Medical and Psychological Support
  • 3. Training for Parents and Teachers
  • 4. Well-being

We want to leave a mark on our children that transforms their lives and helps them achieve their dreams.

To this end, we work with an interdisciplinary team of professionals who are responsible for providing comprehensive support to children and their families, including:

  • NEUROPSYCHOLOGISTS
  • MEDICAL DOCTORS
  • DENTISTS
  • NUTRITIONISTS
  • SPEECH THERAPISTS
  • SPORTS TEACHERS
  • MUSIC TEACHERS
  • ART TEACHERS
  • DANCE INSTRUCTORS
  • SOCIAL WORKERS
  • CHILD ABUSE EXPERTS
